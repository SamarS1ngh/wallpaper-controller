Privacy Policy — Wallpaper Controller

Effective date: 20 July 2026

Wallpaper Controller (the "app") is a wallpaper utility for Android. This policy explains what data the app handles. The short version: the app collects nothing, sends nothing, and works entirely on your device.

Data we collect

None. The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal or device data. It contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs. It has no internet permission and makes no network connections.

Your images

  • You choose images through the Android system photo picker. The app never scans or browses your gallery on its own.
  • The images you pick are copied into the app's own private storage on your device so they can be set as wallpapers.
  • These copies never leave your device. They are not uploaded, backed up to us, or shared with anyone.
  • Removing an image with "Clear all" (or removing a single thumbnail) deletes the app's copy. Uninstalling the app deletes all copies and all app settings.

Permissions

  • SET_WALLPAPER — the only permission the app requests. It is used solely to set your home and lock screen wallpapers.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
