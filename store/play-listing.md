## App title (max 30 chars)
Wallpaper Controller

## Short description (max 80 chars)
Auto-rotate your lock screen wallpaper and set a home image. All on-device.

## Full description (max 4000 chars)
Wallpaper Controller is a simple, private wallpaper tool. Pick a home screen
image, add a set of lock screen images, and let your lock screen rotate to the
next image every time the screen turns off — so each time you wake your phone,
a fresh wallpaper is waiting.

Everything runs on your device. There are no accounts, no ads, no tracking, and
no internet access.

FEATURES
• Set your home screen wallpaper from any image on your device.
• Build a lock screen set of as many images as you like.
• Automatic rotation: the lock wallpaper advances to the next image each time
  the screen turns off.
• "Next now" button and a Quick Settings tile to change the wallpaper
  instantly — the tile works right from the lock screen shade.
• Home image can span across home screen pages (parallax) or stay cropped to a
  single view.
• A built-in event log (Diagnostics) so you can see exactly when the wallpaper
  changed.

HOW ROTATION WORKS
Lock screen rotation runs through a live wallpaper. When you tap "Start
cycling", the app helps you set your chosen home image as a live wallpaper. The
system keeps a live wallpaper running to draw your screen, which is what lets
the rotation keep working reliably in the background without draining your
battery or needing intrusive background permissions. If you turn the live
wallpaper off, rotation simply stops — nothing runs behind your back.

PRIVACY
• No data collected. No analytics. No ads.
• No internet permission — the app cannot send anything anywhere.
• The only permission requested is to set your wallpaper.
• Images you pick are copied into the app's private storage and never leave
  your device. Uninstalling removes everything.

Full privacy policy: https://SamarS1ngh.github.io/wallpaper-controller/privacy-policy.html
